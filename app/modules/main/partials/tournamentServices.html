Tournament Services

Option 1 - GolfersTube will set up our service on a tee box during your Corporate Tournament and record every participant's golf swing from two angles with high speed video. From there the video will be uploaded to our website the same day as the Tournament. Each participant will receive an email with a link that directs them to our website will they will have their own personal account page where the swing videos are stored and could be viewed anytime.
Option 2 - GolfersTube will set up our service on a tee box during your Corporate Tournament and record every participant's golf swing from two angles with high speed video. From there the video will be uploaded to our website the same day as the Tournament. We will then have PGA Instructors from The Leadbetter Golf Academy analyze each participants swing video. Each partisans will then receive an email with a link that directs them to our website and they will have their own personal account page where the swing videos and the swing analysis from a PGA Instructor are stored and could be viewed anytime. (Swing videos are uploaded the day of Tournament and the Swing Analysis will be uploaded within 5 days from the day of the Tournament date)
Option 3 - GolfersTube will set up our service on a tee box during your Corporate Tournament and record every participant's golf swing from two angles with high speed video. From there the video will be uploaded to our website the same day as the Tournament. We will then have PGA Instructors from the Host course where the Tournament is being held analyze each participants swing video. Each participant will then receive an email with a link that directs them to our website and they will have their own personal account page where the swing videos and the swing analysis from a PGA Instructor are stored and could be viewed anytime. (Swing videos are uploaded the day of Tournament and the Swing Analysis will be uploaded within 5 days from the day of the Tournament date)
